Privacy Policy – Daisy Lovers

Daisy Lovers does not collect, store, or share personal user data.

The application allows users to ask relationship-related questions and generates responses using an external language model service (Google Gemini API). The content of the question may be transmitted securely to this service in order to generate a response.

No personally identifiable information is required to use the application.

The application does not use advertising identifiers, location data, contacts, or device tracking.

Some anonymous technical information may be processed by the Google Gemini API service in accordance with Google's own privacy policies.

Daisy Lovers does not store user questions or generated responses on external servers.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you may contact the developer.
